What are Artifacts? Think of Artifacts as a dedicated window next to the chat. 
When Claude creates something - like a diagram, code, or interactive tool - it 
appears there, bringing your ideas and projects to life.



Here's what you can ask Claude to create:

 * Flowcharts & diagrams: Create a flowchart showing the customer journey for 
an e-commerce site
 * Data visualizations: Create a chart from my CSV file that compares my 
different monthly expenses
 * Interactive quizzes: Make an interactive quiz to help me practice for my 
upcoming history test
 * Coding projects: Create a simple to-do list app using JavaScript
 * Learning tools: Make flashcards to help me learn everyday Spanish phrases 
for my upcoming trip to Mexico City
Try this: Ask Claude to "create a decision tree to help me decide whether to 
buy or rent a home 
<https://claude.ai/new?q=create%20a%20decision%20tree%20to%20help%20me%20decide%20whether%20to%20buy%20or%20rent%20a%20home>
